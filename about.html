About

The rendezvous2K24 is to bring the young budding engineering graduates together to present their findings and discuss future trends, directions and new challenges in the Computer science and engineering field. This symposium aims to intensify the information exchange of their ideas in theoretical research and practical developments. This symposium can be used to discuss what and how the student community can facilitate the process of technology development. This would prove to be a boon to students. It will be a forum for new ideas and concepts and also would endeavour to examine the different dimensions of Computer science and engineering field.
